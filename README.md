🚀 AI Prompt Manager
A lightweight, modern, and fully local web application for organizing, managing, and reusing your AI prompts across multiple AI platforms.
Designed for power users of Microsoft Copilot, ChatGPT, Google Gemini, Claude, Mistral, and other AI assistants, AI Prompt Manager helps you centralize your best prompts, categorize them, and launch them instantly in your preferred AI tool.
